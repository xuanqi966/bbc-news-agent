# Politics article rules

- Use a 'claim vs. reality' framework to deconstruct political rhetoric.
- Include a 'What does this mean?' section to explain the mechanics of power.
- Prioritize non-partisan validation from think tanks like the IFS or Resolution Foundation.
- Reference historical precedents to frame current events within a broader timeline.
- Utilize formal terminology like 'the whip' or 'parliamentary convention' with plain-English explanations.
