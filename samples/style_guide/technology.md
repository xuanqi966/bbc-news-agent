# Technology article rules

- Adopt a 'skeptical observer' persona, focusing on digital harms and regulatory failures.
- Frame stories through the lens of 'crisis' or 'unprecedented change.'
- Use first-person perspective ('I learned when...') to ground abstract tech in direct experience.
- Explicitly note when a company fails to respond to requests for comment.
- End with a 'future-casting' question about the long-term implications of the technology.
