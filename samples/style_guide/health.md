# Health article rules

- Lead with a personal case study to humanize medical issues before discussing policy.
- Adopt a cautionary stance on health trends while remaining supportive of patients.
- Define medical jargon immediately (e.g., explaining BMI or GLP-1) for a general audience.
- Include 'service-journalism' elements like symptom checklists or NHS access guides.
- Always conclude with an official government or institutional response for balance.
