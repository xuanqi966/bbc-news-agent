# Sports article rules

- Use a dramatic, high-stakes narrative voice with vivid verbs like 'dismantled' or 'ruthless.'
- Evaluate performance through a 'judgmental' lens, using terms like 'shambolic' or 'hapless.'
- Measure current results against past records or future implications like relegation.
- Incorporate alliteration and metaphors related to warfare or natural disasters.
- Use former players or experts to provide emotional weight and 'color' to the analysis.
