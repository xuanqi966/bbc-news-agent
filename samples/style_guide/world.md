# World article rules

- Open with a thematic hook or cultural reference to humanize complex geopolitical issues.
- Follow a 'micro-to-macro' structure, starting with an individual anecdote.
- Specify the medium of quotes (e.g., 'said on X' or 'in recorded messages') to provide context.
- Include 'contextual callbacks' to remind readers of a subject's past controversies.
- Use precise verbs and descriptive nouns rather than emotive adjectives.
