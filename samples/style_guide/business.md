# Business article rules

- Trace corporate events through a 'ripple effect' on local economies and household budgets.
- Pair official corporate statements with independent data and grassroots personal testimony.
- Use accessible metaphors like 'mortgage crunch' or 'squeezed' to describe economic trends.
- Include 'key facts' or 'what you need to know' sidebars for quick reference.
- Conclude with a call to action for readers to share their own financial experiences.
